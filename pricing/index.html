Pricing FAQs

Do these prices include setup?

All plans include a one-time setup fee to configure TruBiome.AI with your product data, symptom logic, and branding. You can adjust that amount based on the complexity of each client.

Can I change the plans later?

Yes. These tiers are placeholders so you can show structure on sales calls. You can refine the pricing, limits, and features as you validate what works best with early customers.

What if a client needs something custom?

That's what the Enterprise plan is for. You can scope custom integrations, unique logic, or higher usage and quote accordingly.
